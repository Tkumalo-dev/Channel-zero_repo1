About

Channel Zero is a revolutionary platform designed to bridge the gap between unclaimed funds and their rightful owners. Our service helps individuals check for any unclaimed funds from various companies while assisting organizations in locating beneficiaries of unclaimed monies.

Market Research & Analysis

Target Audience: Both companies and private citizens

Market Demand: While it requires significant advertising, unclaimed monies affect everyone.

Data Requirements: Access to company data regarding unclaimed beneficiaries is essential for our service.

Problem Statement

When individuals pass away, they often leave inheritances for their loved ones in their wills. However, some assets may be unintentionally excluded, leading to unclaimed funds that remain undistributed.

These unclaimed assets can include pension monies, stocks, and other financial instruments. Without a centralized system to track these assets, beneficiaries may remain unaware of their rightful claims, sometimes for years or even decades.

Our Solution

Our platform features a dual-sided approach:

  • A dedicated interface for companies to maintain and update their records of unclaimed benefits
  • A user-friendly portal for individuals to search and verify if they are potential beneficiaries using the company-provided data

This creates a win-win situation where companies can fulfill their obligations while individuals can easily discover and claim what's rightfully theirs.

Revenue Model

Channel Zero will generate revenue through a sustainable model:

  • A small service fee between 1-3% based on the claim amount (only charged upon successful claim)
  • Potential acquisition by government revenue services (like SARS) to enhance tax collection through the recovery of unclaimed benefits
